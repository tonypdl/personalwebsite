CONSIDERING A RUN FOR KATY ISD SCHOOL BOARD TRUSTEE

As a dedicated member of the Katy ISD community and someone deeply passionate about education, I am exploring the possibility of running for a trustee position on the Katy ISD School Board in the upcoming May 2025 election.

I believe every student deserves a high-quality education, and I am committed to supporting policies that will continue to improve our schools and foster an environment where students and educators thrive. With my background in education, data science, and community involvement, I aim to bring fresh perspectives and a strong sense of collaboration to the board.

My unique perspective as a teacher without children in the school system allows me to focus solely on the best interests of our educational community. My wife, Jennifer, is a proud Taylor High School alum; she serves the community as a therapist in the Katy area, helping our youth with their mental health and creating a more confident young population in the district. I am dedicated to making data-driven decisions that benefit everyone in the district and creating an inclusive, supportive environment where every student and staff member can thrive.

Over the next few months, I will be engaging with community members, listening to their concerns, and sharing my vision for the future of Katy ISD. Please join me on this journey and stay updated as I explore this opportunity.

Feel free to reach out if you have thoughts or questions or want to connect. Your input is invaluable, and I look forward to hearing from you as I explore this exciting possibility.
