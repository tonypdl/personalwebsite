üçä Policy #1: Cut the State Sales Tax in Half

What It Does:

Lowers the state sales tax from 6.25% to 3.125%

Why It Matters:

Sales tax is the most regressive tax in Texas. Everyone pays the same rate, whether you're wealthy or working-class. Cutting it in half means immediate relief for every Texan, every time they make a purchase.

Estimated Annual Cost:

$15.5 billion

Impact:

  • Average family saves over $625 a year ‚Äî no forms, no delays
  • Boosts local economies by keeping more money in people's pockets

Message:

"A real tax cut that reaches the checkout line. Less taken from you, every single day."
ü™ú Policy #2: End the Sales Tax on Essentials

What It Does:

Exempts the following from sales tax:

  • Groceries
  • Diapers
  • Period products
  • School supplies
  • Clothing under $80
  • Texas-made goods

Why It Matters:

Essentials shouldn't be taxed. Working families pay the most under current law. This change directly lowers the cost of raising kids, going to school, and getting by.

Estimated Annual Cost:

$3.0 billion

Impact:

  • Bigger savings for low- and middle-income Texans
  • Helps parents, students, and seniors on fixed incomes

Message:

"No one should be taxed for buying what they need to live."

Impact:

Message:

"Three weeks a year where your money goes further ‚Äî when it matters most."
