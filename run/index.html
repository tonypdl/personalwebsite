Why Me, Why Now?

Texas families are being squeezed by high costs and a political system that serves special interests instead of working people. As a teacher, data analyst, and father, I understand the struggles of everyday Texans. I'm not a career politician — I'm someone who believes we can do better. This is our moment to choose real solutions over empty promises, and to build a Texas that works for all of us, not just the wealthy and well-connected.

I'm Not Taking a Dime from Corporate PACs. I'm Running for Working Families.

"I'm a teacher, a data analyst, and a lifelong Texan through and through — not a career politician. I'm running because the system is broken, and regular Texans are paying the price."

Texas Family Dividend Plan

Every Texan Saves. No Paperwork. No Red Tape.

  • Sales tax cut in half — from 6.25% to 3.125%
  • Essentials exempted — groceries, diapers, school supplies, Texas-made goods
  • 3 new tax-free weeks — Back-to-School, Winter Essentials, Storm Prep
  • Targeted property tax relief — for veterans, teachers, nurses, first responders
  • No income tax. No fees. No games.
The average family gets $1,000+ back every year. Automatically. No waiting. No forms. Just more freedom in your monthly budget.

Fair Ballot Plan

Let Texans Choose. Let New Voices Rise.

  • Easier ballot access for independents and new parties
  • Legalize fusion voting — one candidate, multiple endorsements
  • Expand early voting to weekends and underserved areas
  • Ban dark money and expose hidden PACs
"Voters deserve more than the lesser of two evils. We make democracy work for regular Texans — not party insiders or billionaires."
